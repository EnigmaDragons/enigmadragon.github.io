ModeaJet Grand Resort

The C.E.O. of the Cloning Mega Corp, Human Perfect, has been murdered at the ModeaJet Grand Resort.
As a bioroid detective you will uncover the illegal dealings of Human Perfect. You will piece together what you believe happened at the resort. Along the way, you will make moral judgments and ultimately deciding who murdered Raymond Soule. The game will let you make mistakes and judge the wrong people. Your choices have consequences!
