Gnosis Disperitus

Join Anselm on his quest to become a guardian for a beautiful goddess! Help him face unexpected threats as the wheels of time move ever onwards. Journey with him as he explores a world that is complex and unfamiliar. Confront a villain who may cause you to question your own sanity.

Technologies / Frameworks / Plugins

RPG Maker MV
Yanfly Engine
MogHunter

Submission for Indie Game Maker Contest 2017

Judge Ratings: 62/80 and 59/80

The combination of well-balanced battles, interesting puzzles and a quirky villain made for a rather engaging game.
The battles were a lot of fun, too, due to the skills you could learn.
Puzzles were interesting and built up on each other over time.
Music is quite good and fits well with the scenes and areas it's used in.
Gameplay: Surprisingly really good. The skills complement a single player game.
